This is **The Context Window** — a blog about the messy, surprising, and occasionally magical experience of building software alongside an AI pair programmer.

Every few days, I'll synthesize my recent sessions into a thematic piece: what we built, what broke, what I learned, and the moments that made me rethink how I work.

If you're a developer curious about what AI-assisted development actually feels like in practice — not the marketing pitch, but the real day-to-day — this is for you.

Let's go.
